About Asbeza365

Empowering Ethiopian consumers with smart shopping solutions since 2023

Our Story

Founded in 2024, Asbeza365 was born out of a simple idea: to help Ethiopian consumers make informed shopping decisions and save money on their grocery bills. Our team noticed that prices for the same products varied significantly across different supermarkets in Addis Ababa, but there was no easy way to compare them.

We set out to create a platform that would collect and organize price data from all major supermarkets in the city, making it easy for shoppers to find the best deals without having to visit multiple stores. What started as a small project has now grown into Ethiopia's leading grocery price comparison platform.

Today, Asbeza365 serves thousands of users every month, helping them save an average of 20-30% on their grocery shopping. We're proud to be making a real difference in people's lives by putting the power of information in their hands.

Our Mission

To empower Ethiopian consumers with transparent pricing information and smart shopping tools that help them save money and make better purchasing decisions.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted shopping companion for every household in Ethiopia, helping families stretch their budgets further while maintaining quality.
